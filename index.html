What this is

"This isn't a photo booth."

You've trained for years for this moment. You've traveled across the country. You're competing at the biggest fencing event in America.

The En Garde Portrait Project exists for athletes like you.

We set up a full portrait studio on-site at Summer Nationals — dramatic lighting, professional backdrop, your weapon and your gear. We photograph you the way elite athletes should be photographed. Not a quick snapshot. Not a posed grin. A portrait that looks like the competitor you are.

Every session is capped and timed so you get real attention, not a 30-second line. Every image is edited to a standard you'd expect from a magazine shoot.

There are 300 slots. Pre-booking guarantees yours.

How it works

Three steps. Zero hassle.

01
Reserve your slot
Pick a date and time window during the event. Pay a $25 reservation fee to lock it in — applied toward your session. Walk-ups pay $20 more and can't guarantee availability.
02
Show up ready
Come dressed in your competition gear. Bring your weapon. We handle everything else — lighting, backdrop, posing direction. Sessions run 15–20 minutes.
03
Receive your portraits
Professionally edited images delivered digitally within 2 weeks of the event. Download, print, post — they're yours.
